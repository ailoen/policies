Data Deletion Instructions

We believe in giving you full control over your data. This page explains how you can permanently delete your account and all associated data from the Samion app.

How to Delete Your Account

You can initiate the account deletion process directly from within the Samion app by following these steps:

Step 1: Navigate to your Profile page by tapping the Profile icon in the bottom menu.
Step 2: Tap the "Edit Profile" button to go to the profile editing screen.
Step 3: On the "Edit Profile" page, locate and tap the "Deactivate Profile" link. You may be required to re-enter your password to confirm this action.

What Happens Next?

Immediate Deactivation: Once you confirm, your account will be immediately deactivated. Your profile and content will no longer be visible to other users in the app.

Permanent Deletion: To allow for account recovery in case of accidental deletion, we have a 60-day grace period. After 60 days, a cloud function will automatically and permanently delete your account and all associated data from our systems This action cannot be undone.

What Data is Deleted?

The permanent deletion process will remove all of your personal data, including but not limited to:

  • Your main profile information (name, handle, bio, email, etc.).
  • All of your image posts, including the images stored in Google Cloud Storage, likes, and comments.
  • All of your logged training sessions and performance test results.
  • Your chat history.
  • Any follow/follower relationships.
  • Your subscription status and history.

Account Recovery

If you change your mind within the 60-day deactivation period, you may be able to recover your account by contacting our support team. After 60 days, recovery is not possible as your data will have been permanently erased.
